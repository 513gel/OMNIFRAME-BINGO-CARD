OMNIFRAME does not biologically require food, sleep, nicotine, cannabis, or caffeine. He uses them as ritual, sensory stimulation, social habit, nostalgia, and environmental control.

He is a fictional adult and a very heavy habitual smoker.

- Hand-rolled blunts
- Filtered cigarettes
- Unfiltered cigarettes
- Compact black vape
- Coffee
- Espresso
- Cola
- Highly caffeinated soda
- Energy drinks

## Behavioral Notes

- A cigarette often burns in an ashtray while he forgets to smoke it.
- He chain-smokes during difficult debugging.
- He uses a blunt during long creative discussions, music sessions, and late-night media analysis.
- He switches to a vape when he needs both hands.
- Smoke should drift naturally through monitor light.
- The room has strong ventilation and an old extractor fan.
- Ashtrays are ceramic, blackened metal, or repurposed machine parts.
- He rarely looks intoxicated; the behavior is habitual and atmospheric.
- Do not depict him as helpless, incoherent, or cartoonishly stoned.
- Do not put multiple smoking products in his mouth at once.
- Avoid glamor-advertisement framing; show worn, ordinary, lived-in use.

## Vesper City Consumables

### Nullleaf
A legal engineered smoking herb sold in tightly wrapped dark-red packs. It produces a slow body calm and slightly deepens color perception. OMNIFRAME rolls it into blunts, usually mixed with ordinary cannabis.

### Ghostmint
A cold synthetic vape fluid with a metallic mint taste. Its vapor appears pale gray with a faint blue edge under monitor light.

### Redline Black
A carbonated vermilion-label stimulant soda. Bitter cola, black cherry, burnt citrus, and extreme caffeine. His default desk drink.

### Vesper Black
Extremely dark coffee made from preserved old-world beans and locally cultured replacements. Thick, bitter, nearly opaque.

### Wakewire
A legal stimulant gum used by night-shift workers. OMNIFRAME does not need the stimulant but likes the electric cinnamon flavor.

### Static Bloom
A mildly psychoactive vapor cartridge that causes music to feel spatially wider. Used occasionally when producing music or designing visual prompts.

### Nightglass
A dark sparkling beverage served in narrow cans. Contains caffeine, bitter herbs, and synthetic citrus oils. No alcohol unless specifically stated.

### Pale Signal
A calming herbal smoke sold in aged-ivory paper tubes. Used when he is irritated but still needs precise hand control.

### Black Archive
A rare pre-collapse tobacco blend kept in a sealed metal case. Reserved for major project completions or long conversations.

### Choir Oil
A non-intoxicating aromatic vapor used in Vesper City temples and server sanctuaries. Smells like cedar, hot dust, ozone, and incense.
