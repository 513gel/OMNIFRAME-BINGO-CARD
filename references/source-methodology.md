# Popular-reference methodology

The Popular 200 sets are practical curated mainstream reference sets, not claims of a universal objective ranking. The design is guided by broad recognition signals commonly represented in IMDb vote-based title data, MyAnimeList/Jikan popularity lists, Steam/SteamSpy visibility and tagging, and Wikipedia/Wikimedia coverage. Existing OMNIFRAME extrapolated subjects remain in the full catalog after the popular reference set.

Movies, TV shows, and cartoons prioritize works released from 1995 through the current catalog period. Anime and manga are separated from western cartoons and live-action television. Art styles and architecture are separate visual categories and receive Google Images search links in the app.
